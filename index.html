The motto of Kendriya Vidyalaya SVP NPA Sivarampalli is तत् त्वं पूषन् अपावृणु (Tat vam Pooshan Apaavrunu). It is a Coed Day School, which services the educational need of hundreds of students from I through XII. The classes in Kendriya Vidyalaya SVP NPA for every academic year starts from April to March.
Kendriya Vidyalaya SVP NPA, Shivarampally, Hyderabad is one of the best Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) schools in Sivarampalli area of Hyderabad and ranks among the top schools in Hyderabad. Here, the dedicated and professional teachers ensure that the children get the maximum out of their education in this Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) school in Hyderabad. The school has given exceptional results in the academic sphere and its students have excelled in extra co-curricular activities too.
